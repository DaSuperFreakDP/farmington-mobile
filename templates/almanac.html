Reading Matchday History

Focus on the TOTAL column for each farmer's points earned that matchday. Here's what each column means:

ğŸ“‹ Column Breakdown

  • JOB: Position assigned for that matchday

  • CROP PTS: Points from harvesting crops
  • TOTAL: Combined points for the matchday

Task Points System

Farmers earn 1 point for passing their task, plus bonus points based on how much their stat exceeded the random roll. For example: if a farmer has 10 strength and the random number is 7, they earn 4 total task points (1 base + 3 bonus).

Crop Harvesting

Successful farmers harvest 30-50 crops, while failed attempts yield only 5-20 crops. Several factors affect harvest amounts:

Harvest Multipliers

  1. Farmer Preferences (1.5x): When daily crop matches farmer's preferred crop type
  2. Injuries (0.4x): Injured farmers have reduced harvest efficiency
  3. Level 1 Catastrophes (0.4x): Environmental disasters reduce yields

âš ï¸ Important: Multiplier Stacking

Multipliers can stack! The order is: preference â†’ catastrophe â†’ injury = final value

Additional Stats Explained

  • CATLOSS: Points lost to catastrophes ("ABC" = Affected By Catastrophe)
  • INJLOSS: Points lost to injuries that matchday
  • INJTOT: Total injuries this season
  • INJPTTOTAL: Total points lost to injuries this season
  • AVG PTS: Average total points per matchday

Catastrophe Levels

  • Level 1: One farmer loses 1 point (minor disasters)
  • Level 2: All farmers lose 2 points each
  • Level 3: All farmers collect no points (very rare)

How to Reassign Positions

  1. Click the dropdown for the position you want to change
  2. Select the farmer you want to move to that position
  3. In the replacing farmer's dropdown, select the farmer being replaced
  4. Click "Apply Changes" to save your lineup
